About Me!

Hi, I’m Sofia Handzy!

I’m a recent graduate from Northeastern University with a B.S. in Robotics and an M.S. in Mechanical Engineering. I love building thoughtful products that make people smile, solve real problems, and add a bit of good to the world.

On campus, I worked on the Mars Rover Team (SEDS) as part of the mobility subteam, and I stayed active in Ukrainian Club and served as the Vice President of Theme Park Engineering Club. I’m happiest when I’m learning, prototyping, and collaborating—whether that’s designing mechanisms, writing code, or soldering a quick fix to get a demo over the finish line.

What I’m into

  • Designing and prototyping mechanisms (gear trains, linkages, motion systems)
  • Rapid problem-solving and iteration: from concept sketches to tested hardware
  • Human-centered projects that are useful and delightful

Outside the lab

I love to cook/bake, craft, and spend time outdoors. In the summers, I teach kayaking at Ukrainian camp which is basically my favorite combination of sunshine, community, and motion control (but with paddles).
